INVESTMENT REPORT 2024 - GLOBAL EQUITY MARKETS

1. MARKET OVERVIEW
Global equity markets showed strong resilience in 2024. The S&P 500 index 
gained 18% year-to-date, driven by technology and healthcare sectors. 
Emerging markets underperformed developed markets by approximately 7%.

2. ASSET ALLOCATION STRATEGY
A balanced portfolio should maintain 60% equities, 30% fixed income, and 
10% alternative assets. During high inflation periods, real assets such as 
REITs and commodities provide effective hedging.

3. RISK ANALYSIS
Market volatility as measured by VIX remained elevated at 22 points. 
Beta of a portfolio measures sensitivity to market movements. A beta 
greater than 1 indicates higher volatility than the market. Sharpe ratio 
above 1.0 is considered good, above 2.0 is very good.

4. SECTOR PERFORMANCE
Technology sector returned 24%, Healthcare 15%, Energy 9%, Financials 11%.
Consumer Discretionary underperformed at 4% due to reduced consumer spending.

5. FIXED INCOME OUTLOOK
The Federal Reserve held interest rates at 5.25%-5.50%. Bond prices move 
inversely to interest rates. Duration risk is critical when rates are rising.
Investment grade bonds yielded 5.2% on average.

6. INVESTMENT RISKS
Key risks include geopolitical tensions, inflation persistence, and credit 
risk. Diversification across asset classes reduces unsystematic risk. 
Systematic risk or market risk cannot be eliminated through diversification.
